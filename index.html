About me

Hello! My name is Iva and I am a student at Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Computer Science and Information Technology Osijek. I was born in Osijek in 1994. I am far, far away from calling myself a photographer (and programmer as well), but as you can see, I enjoy taking photos. Wherever I go I try to capture great shots with my phone. Here you can see different photos in three categories, nature, architecture and other. In the future, I am planning to buy a low budger proffesional camera (recommendations are more than welcome) and spend more time learning about photography.
